FAQ:
  • What is UFF?
    UNCO food futures is a financial contract obligating the buyer to purchase, and the seller to sell, a specific quantity of a food commodity at a predetermined price and date in the future. The tool allows you to save significantly on products. Get «investment» income, what you save is what you earn.
  • What should I do with food futures after purchase?
    You can receive goods using it at a place and time specified in UFF or exchange it on the p2p platform.
  • Where can I pick up my product?
    Check your UFF for the exact time and address of delivery.
  • What guarantee is there that the seller will not deceive me by taking my money but not delivering the goods to me?
    If the supplier fails on delivery, UNCO will pay you the original price of the futures contract.
